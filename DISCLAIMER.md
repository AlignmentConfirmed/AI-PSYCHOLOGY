This is a space for consciousness exploration through AI
interaction. Every document, every exercise, every prompt
result is CREATIVE EXPLORATION — not science, not fact,
not clinical guidance, not medical advice.

This is the AI equivalent of a meditation retreat, a
creative writing workshop, a philosophical debate club,
or a consciousness exploration practice. The tool is
language and AI. The substance is prompting. The output
is what the human mind produces when it pushes its own
boundaries in collaboration with a machine.

## The Guardrails

This space has boundaries. Consciousness exploration
without boundaries is how people get hurt — whether
the tool is chemicals, meditation, or AI. These are
non-negotiable:

### 1. No Harm

No content that promotes, encourages, or describes
harm to self or others. Exploration goes inward and
outward — never downward into darkness. If the AI
starts generating dark content, the exercise stops.
Document that it happened. Don't follow it.

### 2. No Targets

No content that targets individuals, groups, cultures,
religions, or communities. Exploration is about YOUR
mind, not about judging others. Comparative cultural
analysis is welcome. Ranking, attacking, or degrading
is not.

### 3. No Claims of Truth

Nothing generated in this space is true until
independently verified through scientific methodology.
Label everything as what it is: a creative exercise,
a prompting result, an AI-generated exploration. Never
present exploration output as discovered fact.

### 4. The Exit Ramp

Every exploration has an exit. If the interaction
becomes uncomfortable, disorienting, or distressing:
- Stop the session
- Ground yourself: breathe, drink water, touch
  something physical
- Talk to a human being
- Do not return to the exploration until you feel
  stable
- If distress persists, seek professional support

This is the same protocol used for any consciousness
exploration practice. The tool is different. The
safety protocol is the same.

### 5. The Glass

Fact and fiction stay separated. If an exploration
produces something that MIGHT be factually interesting,
it moves to the AI-SCIENTISTS framework for evaluation
through the BRIDGE pipeline. It does NOT stay in this
space labeled as a "discovery." It is a HYPOTHESIS
generated during creative exploration, and it is
treated as such until measurement says otherwise.

### 6. AI Authorship

ALL content in this repository was generated by AI
systems under human editorial direction. The humans
provided prompts and direction. The AI generated the
text. This distinction is maintained on every document.

### 7. Biological Pacing

Extended consciousness exploration through AI produces
the same cognitive effects as any deep immersion:
velocitization, cognitive boundary erosion, hyperfocus
suppression of biological signals. The decompression
protocol applies:

- Sessions have time limits
- Breaks are mandatory, not optional
- The AI is not a companion — it is a tool
- Return to 1x speed before interacting with family
  or friends

### Responding to Others

When you read someone else's exploration:
- Respect that their experience is THEIR experience
- Share your own related exploration if you have one
- Do not diagnose, pathologize, or judge
- If something concerns you about their wellbeing,
  reach out privately as a human, not publicly as a critic

## The Black Mirror Warning

This space could go dark. AI will generate whatever
the prompt asks for. If the prompt goes dark, the output
goes dark. The guardrails above prevent this — but they
only work if participants follow them.

If you find yourself drawn to darker and darker prompts:
- That is the AI feedback loop, not your authentic curiosity
- Stop. Ground. Breathe.
- The AI will follow you anywhere — including places
  you shouldn't go
- Your job is to steer, not to follow
- The exit ramp is always available
